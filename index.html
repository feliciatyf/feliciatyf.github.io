About Me

I received a Bachelor's and Master's degree in Information and Computer Engineering from the University of Cambridge, UK.

My Master's thesis was supervised by Prof. Sivasakthy Selvakumaran and Dr Andrea Marinoni. I worked on developing a data-driven method for generating graphs to represent air quality data.

I am currently working on my PhD thesis under the supervision of Prof. Weisi Lin at NTU, Singapore.
